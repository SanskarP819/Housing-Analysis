## Scenario 1: Overall Data Overview

This visualization provides a summary of the housing dataset including:

* Total number of housing records
* Average house sales price

---

## Scenario 2: Total Sales by Years Since Renovation

This histogram analyzes how house sales vary depending on the number of years since renovation.

Key insights include:

* Relationship between renovation timing and house price
* Buyer preference for recently renovated homes
* Distribution of housing prices across renovation periods

---

## Scenario 3: Distribution of House Age by Renovation Status

A pie chart visualization that categorizes houses based on:

* Age groups
* Renovation status

This helps identify:

* Proportion of renovated vs non-renovated houses
* Age distribution of available housing inventory.

---

## Scenario 4: House Age Distribution by Property Features

A grouped bar chart that analyzes house age based on:

* Number of bathrooms
* Number of bedrooms
* Number of floors

This visualization reveals how housing features vary across different house age groups.
